Our Story

Rooted in Community, Brewed with Care

Cloud Coffee was born in 2020 from my dream: a neighbourhood place where the coffee is extraordinary and no one is a stranger. We opened our doors with two stools, a La Marzocca, and an unreasonable belief that a perfect espresso can change someone's day.

A decade later, we still source every bean directly from family-run farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. We pay above fair-trade rates, visit our partners annually, and put their names on the bag.

Our pastries are baked in-house each morning. The menu rotates with the seasons. The Wi-Fi password is on the chalkboard, and the corner seat by the window is always open.
